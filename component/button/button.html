Button

Buttons are also called as call to action. We have range of buttons and their states.

Solid Buttons

The solid button component is available in three variants: btn-primary, btn-secondaryand btn-danger

Outline Buttons

The Outline button component is available in three variants: btn-outline-primary, btn-outline-secondaryand btn-outline-danger

Icon Buttons

Icon buttons allow users to take actions, and make choices, with a single tap.

Floating Button

Floating-action button is usually fixed to right-bottom corner.
